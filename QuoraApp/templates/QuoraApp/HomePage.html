Is pizza bad for your cholesterol?

Too much saturated fat? The other major concern about pizza is the cholesterol-raising saturated fat. But a slice of regular cheese pizza averages only about two to three grams of saturated fat. A total of four to six grams of saturated fat in two slices of this kind of pizza is actually not much considering the recommended limits for saturated fat are 18 to 22 grams a day, or 12 to 17 grams for those with high blood cholesterol.
